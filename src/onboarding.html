Pro Tips

  • Disable Firefox's "Show editor when saving" bookmark option. This allows the extension to instantly move bookmarks to the right folder without interruption.
  • Keep your bookmark folders organized. This extension not only uses bookmark page contents, but also bookmark titles and folder titles to place new bookmarks.
